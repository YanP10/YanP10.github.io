CSC106 Final Project Page

Here is my final project, a working calculator app. With this app you can use it to help you solve any math problem you might face. From addition to subtraction, to multiplication, and all the way to division. You simply click start, then you can use it just like any other calculator. You click a number, then choose one of the math symbols, and then another number, then click equals, and then it'll give you your answer. You can also hit clear, to reset the calculator incase you wanted to start over with a new math problem.
